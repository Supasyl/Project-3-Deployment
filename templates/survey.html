Privacy

We respect your privacy. This website does not save your information anywhere. We do not use cookies. Once you reload or reset the page the information is gone.
We do sent your information to a function that does the prediction calculation. It is sent using a packed json file, so no information is sent 'out in the open'. To ensure you have complete privacy, we recommend:

  • Chrome: Select 'new incognito window' from the 3 dot menu in the top left corner.
  • Safari: Select 'new private window' from the file menu or use Shift + Command + N.
